1.My favourite Singer

1.1 2NE1

2NE1 (Korean: 투애니원), whose English pronunciation is the same as To Anyone, is the first girl group under South Korea's YG Entertainment. The original members included Bom, Dara, Minzy, and CL. The two music videos for "FIRE" have received a total of 66 million views on YouTube. On June 14, 2NE1 won the Mutizen Song award on Inkigayo, the first number one since their debut, only 28 days after their debut.
